Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Alaska Fairbanks

I work as a seismologist at UAF's Geophysical Institute, in the Seismology and Geodesy group. I am primarily interested in inverse problem theory & uncertainty estimation methods and how to implement them in seismological applications.

My current research is focused on seismic moment tensor and uncertainty estimation, applied to nuclear monitoring. I am an active developer on the MTUQ code.

My work

Moment tensor inversion

I am interested the inversion of moment tensor from waveform data. My work in this area is mostly based on the MTUQ package.

My current main contribution to the code is the implementation of a new stochastic optimization method

  • MTUQ

CMA-ES

I am currently pushing the implementation of the CMA-ES optimization algorithm to MTUQ. This algorithm is easily adaptable to moment tensor inversion, and is very efficient.

Wave equation simulations

I use SPECFEM3D wave propagation software in my projects, in order to calculate synthetic waveforms for the geophysical inversion problems I am interested in.
